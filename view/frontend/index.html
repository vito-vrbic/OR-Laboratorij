Music Albums and Songs Dataset

This dataset offers factual information about music albums and songs, including titles, artists, genres, producers, and more. It serves as an educational resource developed for the Open Computing course at the University of Zagreb Faculty of Electrical Engineering and Computing.

Overview

The dataset includes comprehensive details about 12 music albums and 149 songs. The information is sourced from MusicBrainz, Discogs, and Wikidata, all under the CC0 license.

Data Structure

The dataset is organized into several related tables, detailing albums, songs, artists, genres, styles, and more.

Albums: Information on album titles, release years, genres, styles, types, performers, producers, release labels, countries of relese, songs, number of singles.

Songs: Each song's title, track number, and duration.

People: Names of producers and performers.
